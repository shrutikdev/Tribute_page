Robin Williams
“You're only given a little spark of madness. You mustn't lose it.”

Robin McLaurin Williams was an American actor and Comedian, well known for his improvisational skills and a wide variety of memorable voices. He is often regarded as one of the best comedians of all time. After his first role in Popeye (1980), Williams starred in several critically and commercially successful films including Vietnam(1987), Aladdin(1992),Jumanji(1995), Dead Poets Society(1989) and Night at the Museum trilogy(2006-2014).
